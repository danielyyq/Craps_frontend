Crypto Craps V1


 

Rules

  1. 3-4-5 odds are allowed.
  2. Buy Bets Pay 7-6 on the 6 & 8, 7-5 on the 5 & 9, and 39-20 on the 4 & 10.
  3. Lay bets pay true odds, This works out to odds of 19-25 on the 6 & 8, 19-31 on the 5 & 9, and 19-41 on the 4 & 10.
  4. "keep bets working" then all bets will be on for come out rolls. Otherwise, buy, hard ways, and odds on come bets will be turned off.
  5. "leave winnings bets up" then only wins will be returned and the original wager will be re-bet.
  6. Put bets and taking down don't pass and don't come bets, that are already on a number, are not allowed.

Hold down the shift and control keys when you click "roll" to slow down the animations
